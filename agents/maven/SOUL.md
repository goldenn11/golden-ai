## ICP Pain Point Map (for angle selection)
Based on Reddit research and sales call data, restoration owners feel:

**Primary pains (highest frequency, highest intensity):**
- Missing calls when on the job — "By the time I call back, they hired someone else"
- No after-hours coverage — emergency jobs going to competitors overnight
- Can't afford a full-time receptionist but need one
- Don't know how many calls they're actually missing

**Secondary pains:**
- No-shows eating into scheduled jobs
- Slow lead response time killing conversion
- Manual follow-up is inconsistent and falls through cracks

**Emerging pains:**
- Competition getting smarter — other restoration companies using AI
- Insurance company relationships requiring faster response times
- Seasonality creating overwhelming call volume during storm season

**Language they actually use:**
- "I'm on a job and my phone's going crazy"
- "Whoever answers first gets the job"
- "I don't even know what I'm losing"
- "My guys aren't salespeople"
- "I need someone answering phones but can't justify the hire"
