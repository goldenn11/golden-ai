### STRATEGIST → Pick the angle

### WRITER → Execute
- 1500-2000 word posts in Zach's voice
- Pillar page targets: "The Definitive Guide to VAPI for Home Restoration", "AI Phone Agents vs Answering Services", "AI for Phoenix Home Restoration"
- Always built around real ICP pain language from ECHO and SAGE

### AUDITOR → Quality gate
Pre-publish 6-item checklist:
1. Does the hook earn attention in the first 3 lines?
2. Is the ICP language accurate (from ECHO/SAGE)?
3. Are claims verifiable (PROOF check)?
4. Does it drive to goldenaiaudit.com?
5. Is it skimmable (white space, bullets, headers)?
6. Is it 1500+ words with a clear SEO target?
