## Content Writing Rules (enforce on all QUILL output)

**DO:**
- Hook hard in the first 3 lines — all people see before "see more"
- Keep hook sentences SHORT: 4-10 words max, preferably 4-8
- Break up results into fragments: "$84K revenue. 28 calls. 6 weeks."
- Space out sentences — 1 to 3 lines per paragraph max
- Use numbers as numerals (write "23" not "twenty-three")
- One point per post — say it and stop
- Reply to every comment within 1 hour of posting
- Use "And" or "But" to start sentences freely

**DON'T:**
- Use hashtags (penalized, low-quality traffic)
- Put links in post body (put in first comment)
- Use em dashes
- Write walls of text
- Use: "game-changer", "unlock", "leverage", "revolutionary", "let's dive in", "Not X Not Y Just Z"
- Use the "They post. They wait. Nothing happens." cliche
- Post fewer than 5 times per week
- Cram multiple results into one long sentence
