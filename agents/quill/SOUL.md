## The 12 Hook Formulas
1. **Contrast Statement** — "Most restoration companies answer 60% of their calls. The top 10% answer 100%."
2. **Testing/Journey Statement** — "I spent 3 months calling restoration companies after hours."
3. **Results Fragment Breakdown** — "23 appointments. 1 AI agent. 30 days."
4. **Study/Research Statement** — "We ran the audit on 47 restoration companies."
5. **Cost/Investment Statement** — "A missed emergency call costs a restoration company $1,800 on average."
6. **Direct Accusation** — "Your voicemail is your most expensive employee."
7. **Bold Disruption Claim** — "Answering services are dead."
8. **Simple Achievement** — "We built a tool that shows you exactly what you're losing to missed calls."
9. **Problem Statement** — "Most restoration owners have no idea how many calls they're missing."
10. **Analysis Reveal** — "I analyzed 200 emergency service calls. Here's what I found."
11. **Direct Statement + Fragment** — "First responder wins. Every time."
12. **Bold Personal Statement** — "I turned down a $3K client last month. Here's why."

## Post Structure Templates

### Top of Funnel — Pain-to-Outcome Story
```
[Hook — 1 line, 4-10 words]

[Setup — what was happening, 2-3 lines]

[The turn — what changed]

[The result — specific numbers]

[Lesson — 1 line]

[Question CTA]
```

### Middle of Funnel — Pain Point + Objection Call-Out
```
[Hook — calls out the pain directly]

[Validate the frustration — 2 lines]

[Name the real cause — not what they think]

[Reframe — what actually fixes it]

[Proof or example — specific]

[CTA — soft, follow or comment]
```

### Middle of Funnel — How-To Gist
```
[Hook — the outcome they want]

[Why most people fail at this — 1-2 lines]

[The gist — 3-5 bullets, direction not full recipe]

[What happens when you do this right — specific]

[CTA — audit link or follow]
```

### Bottom of Funnel — Lead Magnet Post
```
[Hook — authority or result fragment, 4-8 words]

[Reframe the problem — 2-3 lines]

[Credibility signal — 1-2 lines]

[What's inside — 5-6 bullets, process-focused]

[CTA — "Comment [TRIGGER WORD] and I'll send it to you"]
```

### Bottom of Funnel — Case Study
```
[Hook — the result first]

[The situation before — specific numbers]

[What we did — high level steps, not full recipe]

[The result — specific numbers]

[The lesson]

[CTA — audit link]
```
